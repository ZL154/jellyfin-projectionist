Internal Library

Hidden Jellyfin library

Jellyfin's player only streams files that are registered as library items. Projectionist creates a library called Projectionist Prerolls at your folder, hides it from every user's home screen via their policy, and uses it internally to serve preroll playback. You should never see it in the UI.

v1.2.0

v1.2.0 features

New in v1.2.0 — per-item opt-out, cinema overlay, coming-soon trailers, post-roll outros, and audio loudness analysis. Existing behaviour is unchanged unless you enable these.

Per-feature opt-out

List of Jellyfin item Guids that should never get a preroll. Useful for kids' films, sensitive content, or items where prerolls feel out of place.
No opted-out items.
Paste a Jellyfin item Guid (32 hex chars, with or without dashes).

Cinema countdown overlay

Show a brief countdown overlay between the last preroll and the feature, like the leader on a film reel.
3–10 seconds.

Coming-soon trailers

Play trailers for other items in your library before the feature — like a real cinema preview reel.
1–3.

Post-roll / Outro

Files played after the feature finishes — credits stings, channel idents, "thanks for watching" cards.
Separate from your main preroll folder.
0 disables. 1–5 chains files after the feature.

Audio loudness analysis

Flag prerolls that are noticeably louder or quieter than the rest of your pool. A loud preroll before a quiet film makes everyone reach for the remote.
Default 6 dB. Files outside ±threshold of the pool's mean are flagged.
File name Mean dB Deviation Flagged

Genre matching in library rules

Each row in the "Library rules" card above now has an extra "Match genres" field. Enter comma-separated genres (e.g. Horror, Thriller) to restrict a rule to items in those genres. Leave empty to match any genre.
